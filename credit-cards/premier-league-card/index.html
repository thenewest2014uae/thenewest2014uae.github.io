Purchase Protection and Extended Warranty

Purchase Protection

Now you can relax when you shop with purchase protection against items you buy on your card. Purchases are covered against accidental damage or purchased items, including internet purchases and items the cardholder gives as gifts. Coverage is valid for ninety days from the date of purchase as indicated on the original store receipt, and is subject to terms and conditions as detailed in the Premium MasterCard cardholder Features and Benefits section on www.mastercard.ae.

Extended Warranty – Purchases are covered with an extended warranty (which duplicates the terms of the original manufacturer’s warranty on covered items) for up to 1 full year from the expiry date of the original manufacturer’s warranty and for a maximum of 3 combined years.

Eligibility

Eligibility criteria

  • Minimum age: 21 years

  • Status: UAE Nationals or permanent residents of UAE

  • Minimum income: AED 8,000 per month

  • Profession: Salaried or self employed individuals


Documents required

  • Completed application form

  • Copy of valid passport and residence visa page

  • Income documents:

For Salaried individuals:
- Pay slip or salary certificate
- Bank statement

For Self-employed individuals:
- Copy of trade license and Power of Attorney / Memorandum of Association
- Bank statement
