Product Detail

Free Flights on airline of your choice

Earn 2.5% rewards on all your purchases. Barclaycard Rewards can be redeemed for free flights on the airline of your choice. Simply book your tickets online or through your travel agent. Call our Phone Banking to redeem and the amount will be credited to your next statement.

For example, AED 40,000 spend = 100,000 points = AED 1,000 refund against any airline ticket purchase; alternatively, you can request for vouchers worth AED 1,000 from a choice of malls across the UAE. List of participating malls:

  • Burjuman Centre
  • Dubai Festival City
  • Abu Dhabi Mall
  • Sahara Centre
  • Sharjah Mega Mall

Cash Back on Dining

Whether it’s a romantic dinner at an upscale restaurant or a cappuccino on-the-go Barclaycard Edge gives you 10% cash back anywhere in the UAE. Avoid the hassles of carrying vouchers or referring to participating outlets.

Every swipe at a restaurant, café or lounge will automatically qualify for 10% cash back upto a maximum of AED 100 per transaction and AED 300 in a month.

The cash back amount will be reflected in your monthly statement.
 

 

Buy – one – get one free at VOX

 

Barclays Edge brings you buy-one-get one free at VOX GOLD cinemas. Imagine yourself in a fully reclining armchair and watching the latest blockbusters on the big screen- and not having to pay for the second ticket!

A Maximum of 3 free tickets per movie session be availed under this offer. The free ticket needs to be utilized along with the paid ticket for the same session and cannot be carried forward to any other session on that day another day.

Participating outlets include: Mall of the Emirates, Mirdif City Centre and Marina Mall (Abu Dhabi).

Offer not valid for Barclaycard transactions made online at VOX.

Airport Lounge Access

Barclaycard Edge offers you complementary Priority Pass, which gives you access to over 600 airport lounges in over 300 cities in 100 countries globally. Now, you can escape the stress of crowded departure halls or flight delays at airports regardless of the airline or class of travel you fly.

You will be eligible for 10 complementary lounge visits annually (calendar year). Any visits over and above these, will be charged at AED100 to your credit card.

The Priority Pass offer is applicable to the Primary Cardholder only.

Unmatched Golf Privileges

Experiences are individualistic in nature. At Barclaycard, we believe in doing our best to help you get the most out of that experience. And with a line-up of some of the finest courses around the world, before your first tee, you know it will be a good round.

Arabian Ranches Golf Club, Dubai

Arabian Ranches Golf Club located in the heart of the Arabian Ranches opened its doors in February, 2004 and was built as a true 18 holes, par 72 desert-style grass course, a signature course designed by Ian Baker-Finch in association with Nicklaus Design.

The luxurious Spanish Colonial Clubhouse was the first in the region to open guest rooms within the Clubhouse, 11 in total. The Clubhouse hosts a fully stocked Pro Shop, with all the latest Golf Apparel and Hardware, a Grand Lobby, and the Baker-Finch Room complimented by a Terrace and the Birkdale Bar, a most exclusive venue to entertain your guests in a striking location.
