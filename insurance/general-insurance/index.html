General Insurance

Motor Insurance

Whether you want to insure your new car or renew your existing insurance, Motor Insurance from Oman Insurance Company (P.S.C.) offers you a comprehensive suite of benefits, competitive rates and superior service.

Motor Insurance comes in 3 packages namely: Silver, Gold and Platinum with varying benefits and premium levels covering diverse benefits.

Product Feature include:

  • Personal accident cover

  • AAA Roadside assistance card

  • Personal belongings cover

  • Natural calamities

  • Third party liabilities

  • Emergency medical expense

  • Windscreen cover

  • Coverage in UAE, Qatar & Oman,

  • Option of availing off-road coverage

  • Replacement of locks cover

  • Valet Parking Theft Cover

Life Shield

Ensure your family's financial well-being by enrolling for Life Shield from Oman Insurance Company (P.S.C.) for as low as AED 1.66 per day so that their future is taken care of in case of unfortunate events beyond your control.

Life Shield Insurance provides a customer with three options to choose from with varying premium and coverage levels.

  • 24 hour worldwide cover

  • No medical tests required

  • 30 days free cooling off period

  • Natural death cover

  • Accidental death cover

  • Permanent total disability cover

  • Hospitalization cash benefit

  • Critical illness cover
