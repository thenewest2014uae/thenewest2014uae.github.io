Ways to bank

Details

Manage your money easily and conveniently wherever you may be in the world.  As a Premier Banking customer, you can enjoy state-of-the-art access to your finances whether in the comfort of your home, the privacy of your office or in one of our luxurious branches.

Premier Branches and Centres

As a Premier Banking customer, you get access to the standard bank branches and service centre as well as the international network of Premier Banking branches.

Experience banking in luxury with our state-of-the-art conference rooms and business facilities, that can be booked free of charge for your important meetings.  Simply sit back and relax while your banking needs are taken care of.

Mobile and Tablet Banking

With Mobile and Tablet Banking from Barclays you can now access many of the convenient features of Online Banking direct from your mobile phone or tablet device, from wherever you are in the world. It’s free to download and use and the transactions you make are secure, effected within seconds and show instantly on your bank statement.

To access our free mobile and tablet banking application you first need to be registered for Online banking. If you are not yet registered for online banking you can Register Online in just a few minutes.

Online Banking

Enjoy a host of banking services at the click of a button, such as:

  • View all your personal banking accounts under the same login including your Investments

  • View transactions details on all your accounts

  • View, Save or Print your statements

  • Transfer funds with the bank and externally

  • Pay utility bills and top up mobiles instantly from your account or credit card

  • Book term deposits online and much more!

Automated Teller Machines/Cash Deposit Machines

Avoid the inconvenience of carrying lots of cash. Enjoy our network of ATMs and CDMs that offer the following services:

  • Withdraw and deposit cash and cheques

  • View account transactions and get a printed mini statement

  • Pay utility bills and top up mobiles instantly

  • Pay Barclaycard balances

  • Transfer funds between Barclays accounts

Reserved and Valet Parking

You are important to us even before you walk into our branches. As a Premier Banking customer, you are assured of parking at our UAE branches through our reserved or valet parking services.
